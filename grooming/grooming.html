Here's a basic tutorial on grooming:

Puppy

Groom

Puppy Grooms are specifically tailored to your pooch with lots of reassuring cuddles. Include:

  • A warm bath with a suitable shampoo
  • Ear cleaning
  • Cliping on request
  • A gentle dry with our now noise dryer
  • Nails clipped

Full

Groom

A double wash in a suitable shampoo for your dogs skin and coat requirements. Includes:

  • Dried using a standard blaster
  • Full brush and comb through
  • Ears cleaned
  • Ears plucked ( if requested and necessary)
  • Cut using your clippers and scissors
  • Paw pads cleared of excess hair
  • Nails trimmed
  • Skin folds/creases cleaned and dried ( pugs, bulldogs etc )
  • Sprayed with a fragrance

Quick

Groom

A double wash in a suitable shampoo for your dogs skin and coat requirements. Includes:

  • Dried using a standard dryer
  • Coat is brushed through and combed if needed
  • Ears cleaned
  • Nails trimmed
  • Sprayed with a fragrance
